Report Online Abuse

Respectfully report any abusive activity encountered online (spam, phishing, malware etc.) to info@schemaxtech.com or fill in the form below.

What we do with abuse complaints

  • First, we determine the type of abuse committed
  • Second, if the domain was hacked or compromised, the domain registrant will be notified. The domain will be suspended if the registrant does not remove abusive content.
  • Third, if it's phishing/malware/419 scam/child pornography, we suspend the domain immediately.

To report any form of abuse activity you can also call us 1 (212) 252-2172
